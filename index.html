My Projects

GIPHY Travel Agency

A GIF related game to travel the world! You can click on buttons with city names like New York City or Chicago and the GIPHY Travel Agency will generate 10 gifs that are related to that city! You can also create your own buttons with any city name in the world! (some results are better than others). By clicking on the gifs, they will animate! Click on the gif again to stop the animation. Enjoy your travels!

Trivia Game

A simple and easy to use movie triva game! The game automatically starts when you open the webpage. It gives you 15 seconds to answer each of the 10 questions. Each question is movie related. All you have to do is enter your answer (1 through 3) in the input box and hit 'submit'. At the end of the game, it will tell you how many you got correct and incorrect. You can click 'play again' to start over. Test your knowledge! Are you a movie master?

Date Night Nirvana

The Ultimate Date Night Nirvana! As a team we created a very friendly user interface that helps you find fun activities in any area in the world! We used APIs from EventBrite, GooglePlaces and YELP to pull data into results pages. By entering a zip code or city, then choosing your desired budget and hitting "find your perfect date", you can scroll or click to each results area to see your available options. You can also click on your results to learn more about your options! Now you don't have an excuse to have a boring Friday night!

Moodsic (under construction)

Moodsic: The music finder to fuel your moods. Log in, click on a mood you're feeling and our Moodsic (mood + music) app will generate songs from Spotify playlists that are in sync with your mood.
